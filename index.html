Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common immigration questions

How long does the visa application process take?

Processing times vary depending on the type of application. Visitor visas typically take 2-4 weeks, while work permits and study permits can take 4-12 weeks. We provide regular updates throughout the process.

What documents do I need for my application?

Required documents vary by application type. Generally, you'll need passport, photos, financial documents, and specific forms. We provide a comprehensive checklist during your consultation.

Do you offer refunds if my application is refused?

We have a 98% success rate, but in rare cases of refusal, we offer free reapplication services. Our transparent fee structure is discussed during the initial consultation.

Can I track my application status?

Yes! We provide a client portal where you can track your application status 24/7. You'll also receive regular email updates on any progress or required actions.
