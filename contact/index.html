Contact FAQ

Quick answers to common questions about reaching us

What's the best way to contact you for urgent matters?

For urgent matters, please call our main number at +1 (555) 123-4567. We also have a 24/7 emergency hotline for existing clients. You can also use our live chat feature for immediate assistance.

How quickly do you respond to emails?

We typically respond to emails within 24 hours during business days. For faster response, consider using our contact form or calling directly. Urgent matters are prioritized and often receive same-day responses.

Do you offer consultations outside business hours?

Yes! We offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends by appointment. We understand that many clients work during regular business hours, so we accommodate various schedules.

Can I schedule a video consultation?

Absolutely! We offer video consultations via Zoom, Teams, or your preferred platform. This is perfect for clients who can't visit our office in person or are located outside Toronto.

What information should I prepare before contacting you?

Please have your passport, education credentials, work experience details, and any previous immigration documents ready. The more information you provide, the better we can assist you during your consultation.
